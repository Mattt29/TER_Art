PROJECT
During our first year of the Master's program, we were assigned a research project.

The objective of this project is to design an architecture able to process a large set of image data and to transform them in order to reveal, or not, relationships between them. To process the images, we used tools studied in class such as dimension reduction and deep learning.
APPLICATIONS
The potential applications of this tool are multiple and could be applied to many fields:
  • Astronomy: quick image sorting for easier analysis.
  • Agriculture: monitoring crop health using cameras.
  • Art: analysis of paintings and detection of counterfeits.
  • Medicine: rapid diagnosis from medical images.
  • Environment: monitoring through satellite image analysis.
  • Industry: improvement of quality control.
  • Transportation: optimization of autonomous driving systems.
  • Archaeology: detection of buried structures using aerial images and LiDAR data.
